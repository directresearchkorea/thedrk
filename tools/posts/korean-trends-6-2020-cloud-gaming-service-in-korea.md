# [Gaming] 2020 Cloud Gaming Service in Korea


  Jay Ahn
* Aug 10, 2020


How is it going to evolve in the market?

For telecom service providers,

1)Lock gaming consumer segment.

2)Be the first 5g platform service provider for future development.

3)Sell more entertainment contents.

1)Use Korea as 5g testbed.

2)Increase awareness.
