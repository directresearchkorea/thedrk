# Cross over to online.

  Jay Ahn
* Apr 29, 2020

I read an article saying COVID19 advances the digitalization 10 years faster. As part of progress, online retail sales exceeded the offline retail sales for the first time last March.

![](/assets/images/posts/aa981d_9fb3893d3f6042e492585f775494bf57_mv2.png)

Thetotalretailindustry dropped -3.3% as offline channels decreased by -17.6% while online channels increased by +16.9%. High user rate of smartphone and high-speed internet infrastructure supported the change. It will last a couple of months under the COVID19 situation. Since consumer wants untact shopping, there is a high possibility online retail would grow faster than the offline even during the recovery time.
