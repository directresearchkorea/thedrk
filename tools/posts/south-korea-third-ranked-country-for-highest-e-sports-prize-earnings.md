# [Gaming] South Korea: Third-Ranked country for Highest E-sports Prize Earnings

South Korea is often regarded as the birthplace of e-sports. And with that said, the country has earned more than $124 million of prize money earnings from 6,826 tournaments, to date. There have been 4,894 Korean e-sports players that have attributed to this, making South Korea the third-ranked country in the world for prize money earnings. We shall be breaking down this list, in terms of the prize earnings, for the top 12 e-sport games.

1. League of Legends: $32,913,241
2. StarCraft II: $22,747,075
3. Overwatch: $17,334,397
4. PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds: $10,009,530
5. StarCraft: Brood War: $6,638,196
6. Heroes of the Storm: $5,818,388
7. Dota 2: $3,906,998
8. Overwatch 2: $3,044,688
9. WarCraft III: $2,241,571
10. Hearthstone: $1,833,932
11. StarCraft: Remastered: $1,517,905
12. PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds Mobile: $1,392,901
