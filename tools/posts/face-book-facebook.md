# Face + Book = facebook.

  Jay Ahn
* Jan 16, 2020

Face + Book = facebook. That's how **hashtag#FaceBookKorea** decorates one of the bright sides in their office. 'Face' means a Korean traditional folding door that could be used as an interior wall separating space or a hidden place. 'Book' sounds a drum in Korean. Drum is an instrument, but also a vehicle to inform something to people. I thought the idea is cool.
