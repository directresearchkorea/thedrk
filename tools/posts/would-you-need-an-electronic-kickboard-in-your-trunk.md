# Would you need an electronic kickboard in your trunk?

  Jay Ahn
* Sep 3, 2019


Hyundai-Kia will have foldable electric kickboard as an option from Y2021. Drivers may keep this in the car trunk and it will automatically recharge from the car. This is another strategy to develop its total mobility service in where they have a gap to the last mile mobility area.
