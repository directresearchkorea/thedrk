# ✈️ Airport Experience Insights: The Top 5 Overseas Airports Loved by Korean Travelers

Which airports truly win the hearts of Korean travelers?

Based on first-hand feedback and detailed analysis, we’ve identified the reasons behind their strong preferences—and the airports that consistently stand out.

🛫 Why These Airports Stand Out for Korean Travelers

As international travel becomes more frequent, airports are no longer just “transit spaces.” They have become meaningful parts of the travel experience—a place where shopping, comfort, convenience, and cultural immersion matter as much as the destination itself.

Korean travelers, in particular, place high value on efficient airport layout, duty-free shopping options, local brands, food quality, and well-designed amenities.

Through DRK’s recent consumer interviews and focus group discussions with Korean travelers, several airports emerged with exceptionally high satisfaction.Among them, five airports were mentioned repeatedly—Changi, Fukuoka, New Chitose, Sydney, and London Heathrow.

Below is an insight-driven breakdown of why these five airports consistently rank highest for Korean travelers.

# 1️⃣ Changi Airport, Singapore

✨ “An airport that feels like a destination in itself”

Changi Airport is regularly ranked as one of the best airports in the world.Korean travelers value Changi not just as a transit point, but as a place where the airport itself feels like part of the trip.

✔ Why Koreans Love It

The iconic Jewel waterfall, indoor gardens, and photo spots A balanced mix of luxury, mid-range, and local brands Duty-free access to Singapore favorites like Bacha Coffee and TWG Tea Entertainment options including a cinema, restaurants, and play zones * Highly efficient design for stress-free transit and wait times

📝 Summary

“Not a place to wait for a flight—an attraction worth visiting.”

# 2️⃣ Fukuoka Airport, Japan

✨ “The best airport for a short, efficient trip”

As one of the closest international airports to Korea, Fukuoka Airport delivers speed, convenience, and reliable shopping options—qualities Korean travelers deeply appreciate.

✔ Why Koreans Love It

Only 10–15 minutes from the city center Expanded lineup of local brands, cosmetics, and lifestyle items Excellent for Japanese snacks and gifts like Royce chocolate Compact but clean, efficient, and traveler-friendly

📝 Summary

“The ideal start and finish to a quick, satisfying trip to Japan.”

# 3️⃣ New Chitose Airport, Sapporo, Japan

✨ “The most fun airport in Japan”

New Chitose Airport stands out for having theme-park-level experiences, making it one of the most enjoyable airports for Korean tourists.

✔ Why Koreans Love It

Royce’ Chocolate World, Doraemon Park, and themed attractions A hub for Hokkaido specialty foods—milk, cheese, and butter-based desserts Numerous airport restaurants including ramen, bakeries, and cafés Domestic terminal far richer in experiences than the international zone

📝 Summary

“An airport where you come for the flight and stay for the food.”

# 4️⃣ Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport, Australia

✨ “An airport filled with Australian character”

Sydney Airport preserves the relaxed, spacious atmosphere that defines Australia—something Korean travelers appreciate right up until departure.

✔ Why Koreans Love It

Abundant souvenirs like macadamia nuts, honey, koala and kangaroo goods Easy access to natural and clean Australian skincare brands Spacious and quiet terminals reducing waiting-time stress Strong lineup of premium wine and whisky options

📝 Summary

“A calm, pleasant finish to an Australian journey.”

# 5️⃣ London Heathrow Airport, United Kingdom

✨ “The peak of shopping at a major European hub”

Heathrow Airport offers one of Europe’s most diverse and premium shopping environments, making it a favorite among Korean travelers.

✔ Why Koreans Love It

One of the strongest lineups of luxury brands: Louis Vuitton, Burberry, Gucci, Rolex A rich selection of British treats like Walkers shortbread, Fortnum & Mason, and fine teas A massive Scotch whisky selection Well-designed terminals that make exploring fun and intuitive

📝 Summary

“Luxury brands, gifts, whisky—everything in one place.”
