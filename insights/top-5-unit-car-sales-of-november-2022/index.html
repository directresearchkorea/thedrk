# [Car] Top 5 Unit Car Sales of November 2022

We are just a month away from 2023. And Direct Research Korea is back again with the top 5 unit car sales for the month of November 2022.

1. Genesis G80: With 5,501 unit sales in the month of November 2022, the Genesis G80 takes the first spot. Its luxurious feel and interior, standard turbocharged 2.5-liter inline four-cylinder paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission seem to have captured the hearts of the Korean public. 2. Porter II: With 5,443 unit sales, it closely trails behind the G80. Available in both diesel and electric transmissions, the convenience it provides for its truck to the user through its 10.25-inch navigation system, electronic parking brakes, and charging ports brings much appeal. 3. Hyundai Avante: With 5,099 unit sales, it takes third place. Through its next-generation design direction, this sharp-looking motor car has secured the 2020 Straits Times Car of the Year for Best Ergonomics and Best Value. It has also the 2021 IIHS Top Safety Pick. 4. Kia Sorento: Its 5,099 unit sales have secured the Sorento in fourth place. Its undeniable capabilities and comfort, convenience, and innovation, along with its wise driver-assist technology have made it a promising deal for the Korean public. 5. Kia Bongo III: With a unit sales of 4,643, Bongo III has claimed fifth place in the month of November 2022. The 181 horsepower, two batteries with a capacity of 58.8 kilowatt-hours, range of 131 miles, and charging from 0 to 80 percent in 54 minutes on a 100-kW fast charger has impressed the public.
