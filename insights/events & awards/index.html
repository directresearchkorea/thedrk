🎮 G-STAR 2025 Review: A Clearer Look at the Future of Korea’s Game Industry

G-STAR 2025, though smaller in scale, clearly highlighted Korea’s game industry trends: a strong return to PC and console titles, rapid expansion of K-game IP, and a shift toward UX-centered playable demos. Narrative-driven development also gained momentum. Despite reduced indie presence and the removed subculture zone, the event reinforced optimism for Korea’s gaming future.

🧴2025 Osong Beauty Expo Review – The Future of Cosmeceuticals and the K-Beauty Industry Seen in Cheongju OSCO

The 2025 Osong Beauty Expo, held at the newly opened Cheongju OSCO Convention Center, highlighted the future of K-Beauty through exhibitions, conferences, and export consultations. Focusing on “Cosmeceuticals,” it showcased the cosmetics industry’s shift toward science-based innovation and expansion into Asia and Latin America. Korean beauty brands engaged with global buyers, building real partnerships. The expo is now a global K-Beauty hub and a flagship event of Cheongju OSCO.
