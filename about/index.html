About Dr.K

Introducing a flexible local team in Korea. We are market × consumer × analytics people with deep expertise across global and Korean markets.

Our Mission

Bridging Global Brands with Korean Consumers

Since 2015, Direct Research Korea has served as the trusted research partner for global brands seeking to understand the Korean market. Our strength lies in connecting deep cultural insight with rigorous methodology.

We combine Delta Code cultural analysis, Deep Persona modeling, and advanced analytics to decode what truly drives Korean consumers — delivering insights that inspire real business action.

Our researchers bring backgrounds from leading global firms including Nielsen, Ipsos, and Kantar, ensuring world-class research standards applied with intimate local market knowledge.
