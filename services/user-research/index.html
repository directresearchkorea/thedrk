User Research in Korea

Digital products succeed when built around real user behavior. In Korea, where digital ecosystems evolve rapidly, understanding user experience is essential for product success.

Understanding Korean Users

User behavior in Korea is shaped by a unique combination of digital culture, mobile-first ecosystems, and fast-moving consumer expectations. Our research helps answer key questions:

How do Korean users interact with digital products and services?
What usability issues affect product adoption?
What motivates users to engage, return, or abandon a service?
How do Korean users evaluate UX across competing platforms?
Methods

UX Research Methods

Usability Testing
Observation of users interacting with products to identify usability challenges and friction points.
User Interviews
In-depth conversations exploring user motivations, expectations, and product perceptions.
Task-Based User Testing
Structured sessions where users complete tasks while researchers observe behavior.
Screen & Interaction Recording
Video-based research capturing real user interaction with digital interfaces.
Longitudinal User Research
Diary studies and ongoing communities revealing evolving behaviors over time.
Industries Supported
Mobile apps, SaaS, gaming, e-commerce, consumer electronics, and more.
