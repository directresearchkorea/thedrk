Consumer Research in Korea

South Korea is one of the most dynamic consumer markets in the world. We help global companies understand Korean consumers, their behaviors, and the cultural context behind their decisions.

Korean Consumer Insights for Global Brands

Companies entering the Korean market often face a key challenge: consumer behavior in Korea can differ significantly from other markets. Through our consumer research, we help brands answer important strategic questions:

How do Korean consumers discover and evaluate new products?
What factors influence purchase decisions in the Korean market?
How do digital platforms and social trends affect consumer behavior?
What experiences make products memorable for Korean consumers?
Methods

Consumer Research Methods

In-depth Interviews (IDIs)
One-on-one interviews uncovering motivations, perceptions, and decision-making processes among Korean consumers.
Focus Group Discussions (FGDs)
Group discussions exploring shared attitudes, cultural narratives, and social influence in the Korean market.
Ethnographic Consumer Research
Observation-based research capturing real consumer behavior in everyday contexts — homes, stores, and digital environments.
Video-Based Consumer Research
Behavioral observation through screen recordings and user interaction analysis.
Consumer Diaries & Communities
Longitudinal research revealing how consumer opinions and behaviors evolve over time.
Industries Served
Gaming, Travel, Consumer electronics, Beauty & lifestyle, Food & beverage, and more.
